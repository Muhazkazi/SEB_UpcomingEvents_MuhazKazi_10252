Event Overview

Welcome to Euphoria 2025, our flagship annual cultural festival that celebrates diversity, creativity, and artistic expression. For three vibrant days, our campus transforms into a cultural paradise featuring performances from talented artists, interactive workshops, culinary delights from around the world, artistic installations, and much more.

Whether you're a dance enthusiast, music lover, art connoisseur, or just looking for an exciting cultural experience, Euphoria offers something for everyone. Join us for this immersive journey through the world's rich cultural tapestry.

  • Live performances from national and international artists
  • Interactive cultural workshops and demonstrations
  • Global food village with cuisines from around the world
  • Art installations and exhibitions
  • Cultural competitions with exciting prizes
  • Night concerts featuring popular bands
All registered participants will receive a festival kit including a wristband, event program, vouchers for food stalls, and exclusive merchandise.

Venue Information

Euphoria 2025 will take place across the Main Campus Grounds, with multiple stages, pavilions, and activity areas spread throughout the venue.

  • Location: University Main Campus, 123 Festival Avenue, Cityville
  • Public Transport: Metro Line A to "University Station" (5 min walk)
  • Parking: Limited parking available on campus (pre-booking recommended)
  • Accessibility: Fully accessible venue with wheelchair ramps and accessible restrooms

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the registration fee?
Your registration fee includes access to all performances, workshops, and activities for all three days of the festival. You also receive a festival kit with a wristband, program guide, food vouchers, and exclusive merchandise.
Are there age restrictions for the festival?
Euphoria is a family-friendly event open to all ages. Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. Some workshops may have age recommendations which will be clearly indicated in the program.
Can I attend only specific days of the festival?
Yes, single-day passes are available for purchase. However, the full three-day pass offers the best value and complete festival experience.
Is food included in the registration?
Your registration includes vouchers for selected food stalls. Additional food and beverages will be available for purchase at our Global Food Village featuring cuisines from around the world.
What happens in case of bad weather?
Euphoria 2025 will take place rain or shine. Most of our major performances and workshops are held in covered areas or indoor venues. In case of severe weather conditions, updates will be provided through our app and announcement systems.
