Event Overview

Get ready for the most exciting tech event of the year! Hackathon 2025 brings together 300 of the brightest minds in technology for an intense 48-hour coding marathon. Whether you're a seasoned developer, a creative designer, or an innovative thinker, this is your chance to collaborate, create, and compete in developing groundbreaking solutions to real-world challenges.

Join us for a weekend of innovation, learning, and networking with industry leaders. Form teams or come solo and find teammates on-site. Our experienced mentors will be available throughout the event to provide guidance and support.

  • 48 hours of non-stop innovation and coding
  • State-of-the-art facilities and resources
  • Mentorship from industry experts
  • Networking opportunities with tech professionals
  • Exciting prizes worth over Rs50,000
  • Free meals, snacks, and refreshments throughout the event
  • Potential recruitment opportunities from sponsor companies
Participants must bring their own laptops and any specialized hardware required for their projects. High-speed internet, power supplies, and collaborative spaces will be provided.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to come with a team?
No, you can register individually and form a team at the event. We'll have team formation activities to help you connect with potential teammates. You can also come with a pre-formed team of up to 5 people.
What skills do I need to participate?
We welcome participants with various skills - developers, designers, business analysts, project managers, subject matter experts, and creative thinkers. Diverse teams often create the most innovative solutions!
What should I bring to the hackathon?
Bring your laptop, charger, any specialized hardware or tools you might need, and your creativity! We'll provide food, drinks, internet, and a comfortable working environment.
Is there an age requirement?
Participants must be 18 years or older to attend. We also have a separate Junior Hackathon for participants aged 13-17 running concurrently in a separate area.
Can I start working on my project before the hackathon?
All code and design work must be created during the hackathon. You can come with ideas and plans, but implementation should happen during the event. Pre-existing libraries and frameworks are allowed.
Will there be sleeping accommodations?
We'll have designated quiet relaxation areas with comfortable seating for those who want to rest. Many participants choose to work through the night, but we encourage everyone to get some rest!
How will projects be judged?
Projects will be evaluated based on innovation, technical implementation, design, business potential, and presentation quality. Each challenge track may have additional specific criteria.
Is there a registration fee?
The registration fee is Rs50, which covers all meals, refreshments, event swag, and access to the venue. Student discounts are available with valid ID. Some scholarships are available for those who need financial assistance.
